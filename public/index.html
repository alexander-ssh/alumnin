Our Story

01
When you study at the university, you become part of the community. You don't just take the course, you experience challenges, fun and achievements. These emotions, these experiences, these trials — they unite for life. 
02
But what happens next? Career, projects, new challenges. Communication with classmates moves to instant messengers and boring university forums, but there is something important missing. Personalities get lost behind avatars and nicknames, conversations blur among the many chats. Groups grow either to huge sizes, where no one is ready to reveal their true selves,or narrow down to a few selected friends.  
03
You want more — to discuss ideas, share experiences, find inspiration and be part of a real, lively big group, as it was at the university. 

That's why we're creating the Alumnin platform, because we know that alumni communication is not just about messaging, it's about energy that moves us forward.

Functions

Authorized Login only

Even if you download the app, access to specific alumni groups and communities can only be authorized by the presidents of the respective clubs and trusted community members. You may have to show your diploma if no one remembered you in class, or come to a face-to-face event.

Full & transparent profile

We have no secrets in the group. Unlike all anonymous messengers, after an authorized login to the group, each participant sees information about other participants, like a resume, only cooler. Even if he doesn't know them personally. This will help you find common topics faster and build more meaningful communication.

A convenient chat

A convenient chat is everything you need to communicate. Intuitive chat where you can send messages, photos, documents, contacts and more. Everything is thought out here to make it convenient for you to share ideas, experiences and inspiration.

Interest groups – Communities

Communicate in the core group of graduates or create thematic groups to share knowledge, discuss ideas, or just chat with like-minded people. Create unique communities that are right for you.

Our benefits

One group we are

With end-to-end encryption, your personal messages are secured. Only you and the persons you're talking to can read or listen to them, and nobody in between, not even us.

Speak freely

Talk and share in groups. Groups managed by alumni presidents & trusted community members to ensure interesting conversations, news, projects, and also mitigation of conflicts and obstructive behaviors to make it safe place to share.

Calendar

Organize meetings and events for your community, as well as participate in global university events: choose a date, time, and location. All the members of the group will see it in the calendar and will be able to join in to make your communication even more lively and intense.

Integration with applications

Share information from different applications, no restrictions, save your favorite articles and documents, maintain your library and share knowledge.
